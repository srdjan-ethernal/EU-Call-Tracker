# AID4SME Open Call #2 - Application Sprint

Deadline: 15 July 2026, 17:00 CEST  
Portal: https://www.f6s.com/aid4sme-oc-2  
Official call page: https://aid4sme.eu/open-call-2/

## Decision to Make First

Pick exactly one challenge and one lead applicant.

Lead applicant options:

- Serbia company: useful if it has strongest product/team and Horizon Europe associated-country eligibility is accepted.
- Malta company: safest EU Member State route if the Malta company can credibly own the solution and costs.
- Slovenia company: EU Member State route, useful if it has the right product/team or can lead a Euro/EU industrial project.

Important rule: AID4SME allows only one application per eligible entity.

## Best-Fit Challenges to Consider

Prioritise the challenge where we can show an existing product, data experience and fast pilot readiness.

- C2.3 Energy system Digital Twin decision support tool
- C3.4 Automated warehouse and internal logistics management
- C4.1 Smart energy management of unplanned machine downtime
- C2.1 Product-production Digital Twins
- C4.3 Semi-automated EV battery disassembly for recycling
- C4.4 Co-bot refrigerator door assembly solutions
- C1.1 Augmented sensing solution

## Critical Facts

- Funding: up to EUR 200,000 per project.
- Funding rate: 70% of total project costs.
- Selected projects: up to 12.
- Duration: up to 14 months.
- Applicants: single SME/startup or consortium up to 2 entities.
- Only SMEs/startups can receive financial support.
- Submission: F6S only.
- Language: English.
- Evaluation: Novelty/Innovation 30%, Impact 30%, Implementation 20%, Team 20%.
