## Recommended Choice Pattern

Choose the challenge where we can truthfully say:

- We already have a working prototype or strong technical foundation.
- We can adapt it to an AID4SME playground quickly.
- We can show measurable resource optimisation or Green Deal impact.
- We can finish meaningful validation in 14 months.
- We have a credible business case after the pilot.

## Shortlist Notes

Use these if our product is AI/software-heavy:

- Energy/digital twin: C2.3 or C4.1.
- Warehouse/logistics optimisation: C3.4.
- Manufacturing planning/forecasting: C3.2.
- Digital twins/process optimisation: C2.1.
- Computer vision/robotics: C1.1, C4.3 or C4.4.
