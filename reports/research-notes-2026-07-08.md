## State and bilateral funds layer

This section adds Switzerland, Netherlands, USA and Sweden. Most of these are not direct "Serbian SME can click and apply" grants. They are routes that become useful when the project structure includes the relevant country entity, national partner, or development-impact angle.

### USA

1. USAID Development Innovation Ventures (DIV)
   - Source: https://www.usaid.gov/div
   - Deadline: rolling / no single cut-off captured in this pass
   - Type: grant / staged innovation funding
   - Fit: evidence-based products or services with development impact. Can be relevant for a Serbian or Maltese company if the solution targets eligible development outcomes and geographies.
   - Route: direct or consortium, depending on DIV rules and project structure.

2. Grants.gov / USAID / U.S. Embassy Serbia monitoring
   - Source: https://www.grants.gov/
   - Type: monitoring channel
   - Fit: USAID and State Department opportunities for Serbia or the Western Balkans. Often aimed at NGOs, implementers, universities or consortia; SMEs may fit as partners or subcontractors.

### Netherlands

1. RVO DHI - Demonstration, Feasibility and Investment Preparation
   - Source: https://english.rvo.nl/subsidies-financing/dhi
   - Type: Dutch government subsidy route
   - Fit: demonstration projects, feasibility studies and investment preparation in foreign markets.
   - Route: needs a Dutch company/entity/partner. Potentially useful for a Serbia or Malta project with a Dutch technology or market-entry partner.

2. Dutch Good Growth Fund (DGGF)
   - Source: https://english.rvo.nl/subsidies-financing/dutch-good-growth-fund-dggf
   - Type: finance route, not a classic grant
   - Fit: Dutch SMEs doing business in emerging markets and local SME finance through funds.
   - Route: requires country/instrument check for Serbia-specific applicability.

3. RVO Starters International Business (SIB)
   - Source: https://english.rvo.nl/subsidies-financing/starters-international-business-sib
   - Type: Dutch SME voucher/subsidy route
   - Fit: Dutch SME internationalisation. Useful only if a Dutch entity or partner is involved.

### Switzerland

1. Swiss SECO Start-up Fund route
   - Source: https://www.seco.admin.ch/seco/en/home/Promotion_Location_Promotion/Export_Promotion/Financing_aspects/Start-up_Fund.html
   - Type: financing/loan route, not a classic grant
   - Fit: Swiss-based investor/company with projects in emerging and transition economies.
   - Route: useful if a Swiss entity or investor participates in a Serbia-facing expansion or joint venture.

2. Innosuisse / Swiss Eurostars-Eureka route
   - Source: https://www.innosuisse.ch/inno/en/home/promotion-of-international-projects.html
   - Type: Swiss national funding for Swiss partners in international innovation projects
   - Fit: international SME-led R&D with a Swiss partner. Pair with Malta Eurostars or Serbian innovation routes when structuring a consortium.

### Sweden

1. Vinnova / Swedish Eurostars-Eureka route
   - Source: https://www.vinnova.se/en/calls-for-proposals/
   - Type: Swedish national innovation funding
   - Fit: international R&D and innovation projects where a Swedish SME or research partner participates.

2. Sida private-sector and challenge-fund routes
   - Source: https://www.sida.se/en/for-partners/private-sector
   - Type: development-cooperation/private-sector partnership route
   - Fit: sustainability, inclusion and development-impact projects, often through specific calls or implementing partners.

3. Swedish Institute funding and grants
   - Source: https://si.se/en/apply/funding-grants/
   - Type: cooperation and network grants
   - Fit: not usually a direct SME grant, but useful for ecosystem projects with universities, NGOs, municipalities or regional partners.
