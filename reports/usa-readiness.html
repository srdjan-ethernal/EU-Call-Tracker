Prvo srediti

  1. Odabrati strukturu: US C-Corp ili LLC, uz proveru SBIR ownership/control pravila.
  2. Pripremiti SAM.gov, UEI, EIN, banku, accounting i IP assignment chain.
  3. Mapirati tehnologiju na 3 agencije: NSF za broad deep-tech, NIH za health, DOE/NASA/DoD za sector-specific R&D.
  4. Napraviti jedan federalni capability deck: problem, novelty, TRL, customer, dual-use, team, budget, milestones.
  5. Pre svake aplikacije proveriti foreign ownership, export control, data/security i US work location zahteve.

SBIR/STTR dokumenti

  • Company registration, EIN, UEI/SAM.gov status and ownership cap table
  • Principal investigator eligibility and employment status
  • Technical abstract, specific aims and work plan
  • Commercialization plan, market validation and letters of support
  • Budget, indirect cost assumptions and consultant/subaward quotes

Najbolji redosled

  1. NSF Project Pitch za broad deep-tech.
  2. NIH ako je digital health / medtech / biotech.
  3. DOE ako je energy, climate, materials or manufacturing.
  4. DIU/AFWERX ako ima dual-use customer fit.
  5. Challenge.gov i Grants.gov kao radar, ne kao glavni sales funnel.
Prioritet 1 Defense customer / procurement route

DIU Commercial Solutions Openings

Fastest non-grant route for dual-use products with commercial traction: autonomy, cyber, AI, energy, logistics, space, sensors.

Eligibility
DIU says it works with US and international vendors from allied countries; each CSO has its own restrictions.
Setup
Create capability one-pagers and map product to active DIU problem statements.

Prioritet 1 Health, medtech, biotech, digital health

NIH SEED / SBIR-STTR

Strong route for biomedical, diagnostics, health AI, clinical workflow, therapeutics, devices and research tools.

Eligibility
US small business route with NIH-specific registrations and topic fit.
Setup
Prepare Specific Aims, research strategy, commercialization plan, biosketches, facilities and human-subjects/regulatory notes.

Prioritet 1 Non-dilutive deep-tech seed funding

NSF America's Seed Fund

Best broad US route for early deep-tech commercial R&D: AI, robotics, semiconductors, energy, medical devices, advanced manufacturing.

Eligibility
US small business route. Start with Project Pitch before full proposal.
Setup
Prepare Project Pitch, technical innovation, market opportunity, team, company formation and ownership evidence.

Prioritet 1 Federal SBIR/STTR gateway

SBIR.gov

Master search for US small-business R&D grants across agencies.

Eligibility
Usually requires a US for-profit small business with majority US ownership/control or qualifying structure.
Setup
Create saved searches by agency and topic; track solicitations for NSF, NIH, DOE, NASA, DoD and USDA.
